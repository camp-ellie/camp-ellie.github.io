Rubik's Cube Demo

Instructions:
Press ' u ' to rotate the U face (top face) clockwise.
Press ' U ' to rotate the U face counterclockwise

Press ' r ' to rotate the R face (right face) clockwise.
Press ' R ' to rotate the R face counterclockwise

Press ' l ' to rotate the L face (left face) clockwise.
Press ' L ' to rotate the L face counterclockwise

Press ' d ' to rotate the D face (bottom face) clockwise.
Press ' D ' to rotate the D face counterclockwise

Press ' f ' to rotate the F face (front face) clockwise.
Press ' F ' to rotate the F face counterclockwise

Press ' b ' to rotate the B face (back face) clockwise.
Press ' B ' to rotate the B face counterclockwise
